Visual Analysis of Spatio-temporal Phenomena with 1D Projections

This is a live version of the prototype produced for the publication Visual Analysis of Spatio-temporal Phenomena with 1D Projections by Max Franke, Henry Martin, Steffen Koch, and Kuno Kurzhals; submitted to and presented at EuroVis 2021 held virtually in Zurich, Switzerland, on June 14–18, 2021. The code for this prototype is available under the Apache-2.0 license on GitHub.

The COVID-19 data was obtained from Covidatlas and is in the public domain. The Australian wildfire data was obtained from MODIS Collection 6 NRT Hotspot / Active Fire Detections MCD14DL distributed from NASA FIRMS (DOI: 10.5067/firms/modis/mcd14dl.nrt.006)

COVID-19 Cases

The first dataset is a timeseries of confirmed COVID-19 cases. The data is hierarchically aggregated based on geopolitical entities: countries, states or provinces, counties, and on the lowest level cities.

The data originates from multiple sources, which have been aggregated by https://covidatlas.com/.

Australian Wildfires

Satellite imagery measured Fire Radiative Power (FRP) over Australia, indicating wildfires. These are visualized on a map and in a 1D sequence over time. The data is hierarchized based on grid squares.

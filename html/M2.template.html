M2: Neighborhood Preservation

This metric measures how well a k-neighborhood in the high-dimensional (i.e., geospatial) domain is preserved in the low-dimensional (i.e., projected timeline) domain. The k is chosen based on the data. The measure is normalized to the interval [0, 1], where a value of 1 means perfect preservation of neighborhood.

Venna, J., & Kaski, S. (2001, August). Neighborhood preservation in nonlinear projection methods: An experimental study. In International Conference on Artificial Neural Networks (pp. 485-491). Springer, Berlin, Heidelberg.
